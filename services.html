What we deliver.

End-to-end embedded systems services from initial concept through production deployment. Every engagement is tailored to your specific platform, timeline, and regulatory requirements.

⬡ Firmware Development

C / C++STM32ESP32FreeRTOSBare Metal

We write production-grade firmware for microcontroller platforms ranging from simple 8-bit controllers to complex ARM Cortex-M7 systems. Our code is structured for maintainability, testability, and long-term support.

  • Bare-metal and RTOS-based architectures (FreeRTOS, Zephyr)
  • Peripheral drivers: ADC, DAC, SPI, I²C, UART, CAN, USB
  • Bootloader design and over-the-air (OTA) update systems
  • Power management and low-power sleep mode optimization
  • Automated unit testing and hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) test frameworks

◈ Systems Integration

BLECAN BusSPII²CModbusRS-485

Connecting sensors, actuators, displays, and communication interfaces into a cohesive system. We handle the full signal chain from transducer to cloud.

  • Multi-sensor data fusion and real-time signal processing
  • Wireless protocol stacks: BLE, WiFi, Zigbee, LoRa
  • Industrial bus integration: CAN, Modbus RTU/TCP, RS-485
  • Gateway and bridge design between protocols
  • System-level debugging with logic analyzers and protocol decoders

⬢ PCB Design

KiCadAltiumDFMSignal Integrity

From schematic capture through board layout, we design PCBs that work the first time. Every design considers manufacturability, testability, and EMC compliance from the start.

  • Schematic capture and component selection
  • Multi-layer PCB layout with controlled impedance
  • Power supply design: switching regulators, LDOs, battery management
  • Design for Manufacturing (DFM) and Design for Test (DFT)
  • BOM optimization and vendor management

◉ HMI & Display Systems

.NET MAUIWPFNextionAndroidWearOS

The interface between your embedded hardware and the humans who use it. We build touchscreen UIs, desktop dashboards, and mobile companion apps that present complex sensor data clearly.

  • Nextion HMI panel design and firmware integration
  • .NET MAUI cross-platform mobile apps (Android/iOS)
  • WPF desktop dashboards for monitoring and control
  • Real-time data visualization with ring gauges, charts, and alerts
  • BLE and USB connectivity between app and hardware
