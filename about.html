// The Company

From embedded systems
to artificial intelligence.

Folger AI Systems LLC is the parent company behind DevPulse.Tools, ByteOven, ByteSMTP, GovSeeker, and SiteCrisp — five products solving real problems for developers, teams, businesses, and government contractors.

Founded by a systems engineer with 24+ years shipping real hardware: surgical robotics (FDA-aware development), aerospace pilot monitoring, industrial UV curing and process control, and ten years running Folger Technologies, a 3D printer manufacturer. That background shapes everything — when your code has to work inside a medical device, "good enough" isn't a phrase you use.

Now applying that same engineering discipline to AI software. Every product runs in production, serves real users, and earns its existence. The stack spans Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, Docker, and Kotlin for Android — self-hosted, self-operated, no middlemen.

Our Approach

  • Build products that solve real problems, not chase trends
  • Ship fast, iterate based on real user feedback
  • Privacy-first — respect user data by default
  • Make powerful technology accessible and affordable
  • Open source where it makes sense
