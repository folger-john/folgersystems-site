// The Company

From embedded systems
to artificial intelligence.

Folger AI Systems LLC is an AI-focused technology company and the parent organization behind DevPulse.Tools, ByteOven, ByteSMTP, and GovSeeker.

The company was founded by a systems engineer with over two decades of hands-on experience shipping products across medical robotics, aerospace, industrial automation, and additive manufacturing. That background — building things that have to work in the real world — shapes everything we build today.

Now we're applying that same ship-it mentality to AI. Every product we build solves a real problem, ships fast, and works reliably. No vaporware. No hype. Just useful tools powered by modern AI.

Our Approach

  • Build products that solve real problems, not chase trends
  • Ship fast, iterate based on real user feedback
  • Privacy-first — respect user data by default
  • Make powerful technology accessible and affordable
  • Open source where it makes sense
